Science and Technology

What is Science?

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observationand experimentation. Science is split into two:

  • Natural Science
  • Applied Science
Natural science can be broken down into fields such as:
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • Astronomy
  • Meteorology
  • Geology

What is Technology?

Technology is the appliaction of scientific knowledge, skills, methods and equipment to manufacture goods and services to satisfy human needs. The advancement of technology has hugely benefitted our world from easy access to information on the WWW to being able to communicate with peoples all over th globe. However it has come with is drawbacks; addiction, cybercrime, access of explicit content to minors, laziness etc.

Differences Between Science and Technology

Science
Technology
1. Science is the study of the natural world through observation and experimentation.
Technology is the appliaction of scientific know-how to produce goods and services to satisfy human needs.
2. Science does not change; only additions are made.
Technology is ever-changing
3. The goal of Science is to understand natural phenomena
The goal of Technology is to solve real-world problems
